NY families still recovering from 2008 recession face economic uncertainty

By Elizabeth Turley

"As of January 1, 2008, most households applying for SNAP benefits no longer have to pass a savings/resource test in order to get SNAP benefits. This means having money in a savings, checking or retirement account, or having other resources, will not keep you from being eligible for SNAP benefits, as long as you meet the income guidelines."

Source: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) Caseloads and Expenditures: Beginning 2002, Annual Population Estimates for New York State and Counties: Beginning 1970
